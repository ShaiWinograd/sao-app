# 1. Foundations and Navigation

## Worker roles

A worker may be eligible as:

- `עובדת`
- `ראש צוות`

Role eligibility belongs to the worker profile, but the actual role is assigned per shift.

When a shift requires a team leader:

- One position is reserved for `ראש צוות`.
- The first eligible team leader who successfully joins that reserved position receives the team-leader role.
- Other team-leader-eligible workers may fill regular worker positions.
- They are displayed as `עובדת` for that shift.

## Worker dashboard

Show:

- Next assigned shift
- Pending direct assignments requiring acceptance
- Open jobs
- Swap requests requiring response
- Missing clock-in or clock-out actions
- Missing end-of-shift forms
- Monthly report awaiting review
- Recent notifications

## Worker-visible information

Workers may see:

- Customer name
- Exact address
- Job type
- Date and hours
- Assigned workers
- Their own shift role
- Worker-visible project and job notes
- Required forms
- Their own attendance
- Their own hourly rate and fixed daily payment
- Their own monthly reports

Workers may not see:

- Customer pricing
- Other workers' rates
- Other workers' phone numbers
- Owner-only notes
- Customer phone number unless assigned as `ראש צוות`
- Owner-only worker log

## Team-leader initial permissions

The assigned team leader may see:

- Customer phone number
- Full worker-visible project and customer instructions
- Assigned worker names
- Issue-reporting action
- Dedicated team-leader end-of-shift form

Additional team-leader permissions remain future scope.
